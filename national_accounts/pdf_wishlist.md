## Priority 1: Critical (Would Fill Major Gaps)

### 1. Ayres (1939) - Business Activity Index
- **Full Title**: "Turning Points in Business Cycles" by Leonard P. Ayres
- **Publisher**: Macmillan, 1939
- **Used For**: Series S004, S005, S006 (Business Cycles 1831-1939)
- **Why Needed**: Only source for pre-NBER business cycle data
- **Figures Affected**: Fig2.4A, Fig2.4B, Fig2.4C

### 2. Warren & Pearson - Historical Wholesale Prices
- **Full Title**: "Prices" by George F. Warren and Frank A. Pearson
- **Publisher**: Wiley, 1933
- **Used For**: Series S010, S011 (US Wholesale Prices 1780-1890)
- **Why Needed**: Primary source for pre-BLS price data
- **Figures Affected**: Fig2.8, Fig2.9, Fig5.3, Fig5.4

### 3. Stigler & Kindahl (1970) - Industrial Prices
- **Full Title**: "The Behavior of Industrial Prices" by George J. Stigler and James K. Kindahl
- **Publisher**: NBER, 1970
- **Used For**: Price concentration analysis (Chapter 8)
- **Why Needed**: Industrial price behavior during 1957-1966
- **Figures Affected**: Fig8.1

## Priority 2: Important (Would Improve Coverage)

### 4. Ibbotson SBBI Yearbook (Latest)
- **Full Title**: "Stocks, Bonds, Bills, and Inflation Yearbook"
- **Publisher**: Ibbotson Associates / Morningstar

- **Full Title**: "Capital in the American Economy: Its Formation and Financing"
- **Publisher**: Princeton University Press, 1961
- **Used For**: Pre-1929 capital stock estimates
- **Why Needed**: Historical capital formation context
- **Figures Affected**: Various Chapter 6 figures

### 6. Goldsmith (1955-56) - National Wealth
- **Full Title**: "A Study of Saving in the United States" (3 volumes)
- **Publisher**: Princeton University Press
- **Used For**: Historical wealth distribution
- **Why Needed**: Pre-war wealth estimates
- **Figures Affected**: Various Chapter 16 figures

- **Used For**: Historical business cycle analysis

- **Full Title**: "Measuring Business Cycles"

- **Full Title**: "Manpower in Economic Growth"
- **Publisher**: McGraw-Hill
- **Used For**: Historical labor force/employment
- **Why Needed**: Pre-BLS employment estimates

## Search Tips

**For older economics books:**
- Internet Archive (archive.org) - Free
- HathiTrust - Free for public domain
- JSTOR - Institutional access
- Google Books - Preview available
- WorldCat - Library locations

**For NBER publications:**
- nber.org/books-and-chapters (some free PDFs)
- Many pre-1960 NBER books are public domain
